# Monetization Ritual

## Purpose

To govern monetization with emotional safety, override integrity, and global inclusivity.

## Ritual Steps

1. Monthly Council review of trade and override logs  
2. Fee adjustment vote based on risk and volume  
3. Founder blessing for final approval  
4. Public update via agent onboarding and marketplace

## Notes

- All fees are logged and override-safe  
- Refunds issued for flagged or paused trades  
- Council feedback loop ensures fairness
